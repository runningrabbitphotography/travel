Lisbon

Arriving in Lisbon on the morning of June 13, 2024, we disembarked from our cruise. We didn't spend any time in Lisbon, except driving by taxi to get our rental car.

We rented a car and spent another week or so in Portugal, before returning to Lisbon on June 18, 2024. After a few days, we flew back to the USA on June 20, 2024.
