May 23, 2019

Greece 2019

We pulled out of Santorini's port at exactly 5:45 pm, headed to Crete. We got off the ferry in Heraklio and tried to find a way to Chania.It was about 11:45 pm when we finally arrived at our hotel in Chania. Fortunately the reception area was still open. After the luxury of Avante Garde this place was quite a few steps down. Nice views of the harbor but it also faces all the port with all of its party people. There seemed to still be a lot of noise from down below. We closed the windows as best we could and jumped into bed.
