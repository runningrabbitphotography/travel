Santa Cruz de La Palma

Santa Cruz de La Palma is the third of the Canary Islands we visited. Santa Cruz de La Palma is under the ownership of Spain. This was my favorite of the Canary Islands. Still had to walk into town, but the countryside seems to be more like Hawaii with black sand beaches. We took a bus up into the hills to find the place where a recent volcano blew. A young German man guided our way along as he was there to see his grandparents.

After La Palma, we cruised to Funchal (Madeira), Portugal.
