Stavanger

We boarded the Holland America, Nieuw Statendam, cruise ship on Sunday, May 14, 2023 for a 14 day cruise up the coast of Norway. We stopped in the ports of Stavanger, Flam, Trondheim, Alesund, Hammerfest, Honningsvag, Geiranger and Bergen. We returned to Amsterdam on Sunday, May 28 2023 and took a train to stay in Delft for 3 days before flying back to San Francisco.

Our first port of call was Stavanger on Tuesday, May 16, 2023.
