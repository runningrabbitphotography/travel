Southwest USA 2021

We left Mesa Verde National Park and Canyon de Chelly snd stopped in Sedona, AZ. We stayed at a B&B called The Penrose, which was absolutely the best. We took numerous hikes, one of which was walking to the top of Devil's Bridge. There, we walked out on the narrow and precipitous bridge, high above the floor below.
