Pinhao

We used a rental car to drive to Pinhao, Portugal. After a number of hours and a very frustrating and exhausting drive, we ended up at our hotel at Casa de Casal de Loivos in Pinhao. It is a very difficult place to find. Not sure if it was really worth the effort but the views were incredible. Pinhao and the surrounding hills of vineyards is where the grapes for Port wine are grown and processed, before being shipped by boat to Porto, Portugal.
