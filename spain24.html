June 3, 2024

Barcelona

There is a central point in Barcelona with a tall statue of Christopher Columbus. That is where the cruise shuttle bus dropped us off. Then, it is a short walk up Las Ramblas to the center of town and the Gothic district. Although a short walk to Las Ramblas, it was extremely crowded and noisy. There was a lot of construction going on and the hoards of people made it a difficult walk.

After Barcelona, we cruised the Alboran Sea and ended up in Cadiz, Spain.
