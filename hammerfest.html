Hammerfest

Our fifth port of call was Hammerfest on Sunday May 21, 2023. The previous day we spent cruising the sea on our way to Hammerfest. Located towards the northern tip of Norway, Hammerfest is where we started noticing the Sun never setting, the presence of oil rigs and the appearance of reindeer. In Hammerfest it was Sunday, so there wasn't a whole lot going on.
