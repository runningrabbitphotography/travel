June 15, 2024 - June 18, 2024

Porto

From Pinhao we wound our way down through the Duro valley to Porto, where the Port wine is shipped out to all the world. This is where you will find the well-known Port wines of Caleb, Sandeman and Kokpe. We had another extremely difficult time finding the Hertz rental car return. Running low on gas we finally came down the street in front of the Hertz rental office.

We took our suitcases in hand and proceeded to find our hotel. Easier said than done. Unfortunately, it was also in Porto where we both got very sick and had to curtail our activities.
