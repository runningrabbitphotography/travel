Sintra

While I had my downtime in Porto, Janet took a train ride to Sintra for a day trip. Sintra is usually very crowded, unless you can get there early in the morning. Looks like a wonderful place, although a bit Disney in nature.

After Sintra, Janet returned to Lisbon and we went to dinner. Next day, June 20, 2024, back to the USA.
