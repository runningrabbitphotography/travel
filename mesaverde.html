Southwest USA 2021

We left the Grand Canyon for Mesa Verde, CO. Once in Mesa Verde National Park, we drove up a long and winding rode to our hotel. The only hotel in the Park. The next few days we spent viewing the various cliff dwellings of the Ancestral Puebloans, around 1200 CE.
