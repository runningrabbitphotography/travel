Honningsvag

Our sixth port of call was Honningsvag on Monday May 22, 2023. Located towards the northern tip of Norway, like Hammerfest, the Sun never really sets, there are obviously more signs of the oil industry and more reindeer this time of year. Both Hammerfest and Honningsvag share a rivalry about which city is the farthest north. It all depends on population size and the definition of a city. That has changed over time between the two.

We spent the next 2 days, Tuesday May 23, 2023 and Wednesday May 24, 2023 traveling at sea heading south to our next port.
