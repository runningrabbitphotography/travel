June 4, 2024

Cadiz

Time was spent cruising the Alboran Sea on June 4, 2024.

On June 5, 2024, we stopped in Cadiz. We had arranged for an excursion, fully guided, to take us to Seville. We made it to Seville and drove through parts of the downtown with the guide pointing out all the buildings that were built during the Exposition years long ago. It reminds us a lot of Balboa Park in San Diego. The excursion took almost all day.

After Cadiz, we cruised to Casablanca, Morocco.
