Santa Marinella

Santa Marinella is south of Civitavecchia. We took a train to Santa Marinella and walked around by the beach quite a bit and stopped a couple of times to have some refreshments and celebrate Holly's birthday. We stopped to have gelato and something to drink. Wine for Janet and beer for me. Nice beach town, but a little too early in the season to see many beach goers.
