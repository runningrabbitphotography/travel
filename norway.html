Amsterdam

We flew from San Francisco to Amsterdam in the Netherlands. Arrived on Thursday May 11, 2023, visited Utrecht and stayed in Amsterdam until the cruise started on Sunday May 14, 2023. We ended the cruise back in Amsterdam, took a train to stay in Delft, visited Gouda, returned to Delft and stayed there until we flew back to San Francisco on Wednesday May 31, 2023.

Amsterdam was the first stay on our vacation.
