Greece 2019

We left Naxos about 1:55 pmÂ aboard Blue Star Ferries on our trip to Santorini. The first place you pass at Santorini is specifically for the cruise ships. They ascend directly up into Thira, which is the capitol. Further on is the second place to disembark which is specifically for the ferries. That's where we disembarked. We finally made it to the top and proceeded to the town of Thira where the public bus station is located. From there we took the next public bus going to Akrotiri which is where our hotel, Avant Garde, was. We arrived inside the hotel and registered. We were taken to our room and were just blown away. This place has incredible views of the caldera and the ocean. Our room is huge and looks directly out at the caldera. It is more of a suite, with a patio. There are two pools. One outdoors and one indoors. Our room also has its own jacuzzi outside. We were given a complimentary bottle of wine to start off with.
