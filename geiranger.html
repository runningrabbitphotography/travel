May 25, 2023

Geiranger

After leaving Honningsvag, we spent 2 days traveling at sea on our way south to our next port. Our seventh port of call was Geiranger, arriving on Thursday May 25, 2023. We traveled through the Geirangerfjord fjord on the way to Geiranger. There must have been hundreds of waterfalls coming down the cliffs on both sides of the fjord. Geiranger is another one of those small towns located at the end of the fjord.
