Delft

We left Bergen and had a sea day before arriving back in Amsterdam. After disembarking from our cruise in Amsterdam on Sunday, May 28, 2023, Janet and I took a train to the city of Delft. This is where we would spend 3 days before boarding the jet plane back to San Francisco on Wednesday, May 31, 2023. While in Delft, we took a side trip to Gouda, but just for the day.
