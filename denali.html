June 24, 2018

Alaska 2018

We both woke up around 4:00am after cruising all night just in time to see the ship start to dock in Seward. We took off by bus around 7:45am and headed out on our way to Denali. It was a long trip, all totaling around 9 hours. Finally, we made it to our resort hotel in Denali. Even though we were with Holland America, we were booked at the Princess Resort. Next morning we made our way over to where we caught the Denali National Park bus to tour Denali. It lasted about 9 hours.
