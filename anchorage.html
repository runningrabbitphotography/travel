June 25, 2018

Alaska 2018

We left in the morning for Anchorage on a train to Talkeetna and Anchorage. We were the last car of the train, so we were able to stand out on back if we wanted to. We all actually sat up on top. The canopy was almost all glass so we had really good views of everything. It was nice to step downstairs and go out back for better pictures.
