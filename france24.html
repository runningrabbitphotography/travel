Saint-Tropez

This was our first port of call on the cruise. Saint-Tropez is mainly known for its wealthy residents and their overly lavish yachts that fill the harbor. The town caters to tourists and has mainly high end shops and restaurants. We walked around looking for interesting architecture and went through quite a few of the shops but mainly played the tourist.

There was one fancy dessert/pastry that we heard of so we found a place that had it. It was called Tarte Topezienne and was very tasty. Actually, it was very typically pastry and cream filling with raspberries.

After Saint-Tropez, we cruised to Barcelona, Spain.
