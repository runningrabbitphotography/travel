Arrecife (Lanzarote)

After leaving Agadir, Morocco, we stopped at Arrecife as the first of the Canary Islands we visited. Arrecife (Lanzarote) is under the ownership of Spain. We had to take a shuttle into town from the ship. The town is very nice and a lot less congested than Casablanca, for example.

After Arrecife, we cruised to Santa Cruz de Tenerife.
