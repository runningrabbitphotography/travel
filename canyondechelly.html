Southwest USA 2021

We left Mesa Verde National Park heading for Sedona, AZ. Along the way we took a slight detour to visit Canyon de Chelly National Monument in Arizona. Canyon de Chelly was the subject of one of my books, The Ellie Mae. So, we decided to take a quick look at the canyon and it's cliff dwellings earlier in time than Mesa Verde's.
